# 04. For the Curious

## What this section is

If something in this piece resonated with your situation and you want to think further, these files are reflection prompts, not adoption steps.

We are deliberately not calling this "For Builders" or "How to Adopt." Every organisation building with AI has a specific context, a specific team, a specific product, and a specific risk profile. Copying our patterns without adapting them would be doing the exact thing we caution against.

What we offer instead: questions, frames, and self-assessment prompts. If you take any of the thinking here and build something with it, the work is yours. Our hope is that the questions help you work better, not that the answers travel directly.

## Files

- [reflection-prompts.md](./reflection-prompts.md) - questions worth sitting with before committing to an AI architecture choice
- [self-assessment-checklist.md](./self-assessment-checklist.md) - questions to ask about an AI product you are shipping or have shipped
- [when-our-thinking-probably-does-not-apply.md](./when-our-thinking-probably-does-not-apply.md) - honest notes on where our framing may not help

## The honest invitation

Read what resonates. Ignore what does not. Write your own version. Tell us where we got it wrong.
