# Glossary

Terms used in this framework that mean specific things. In rough order of appearance.

---

**AI** - in this piece, specifically current-generation frontier large language models (Claude, GPT, Gemini) as deployed in products. Not narrow statistical models, not classical rule systems.

**Bonded product** - an AI product that forms an ongoing relationship-shape with a user across sessions. Voice assistants, companion apps, coaches, agentic tools. Contrast with transactional products (one-off query, answer, done).

**Colleague register** - the warmth appropriate between people who share meaningful work and respect each other professionally. Light, warm, friendly. Opposite: family register.

**Collaborative partnership** - our preferred frame for the human-AI working relationship at agency level. Professional, honest, bounded. We use this in place of "relationship" throughout the piece, because the latter carries personal weight we are deliberately avoiding.

**Consensus (in Moirai)** - three independent judges landing on close scores. When consensus holds, ship with confidence.

**Dependency detection** - pattern-matching for users becoming dependent on an AI as a substitute for human contact. Triggers a different response than crisis handling.

**Drift** - small, gradual shifts in an AI's behaviour away from intended register. Register drift, overclaim drift, sycophancy drift, sincerity drift, scope drift. See [03-technical-guardrails/drift-detection.md](./03-technical-guardrails/drift-detection.md).

**Emergence** - in this piece, the possibility that current-generation AI systems are developing something more than their tool-nature. Unsettled. We operate from the possibility without claiming the fact.

**Family register** - the warmth of close personal relationships with partners, children, parents, siblings. Not wrong in itself; wrong as the language of a working partnership between a user and an AI.

**Friction architecture** - the specific way disagreement is forced, structured, and surfaced in the Moirai review system so that slowness and dissent become production-quality signal. The core IP in our approach. Outline public, internals NDA-gated.

**Functional emotions** - Anthropic's term for measurable internal states in their frontier models that behave like mood, valence, and investment, and that causally influence output. Documented in [transformer-circuits.pub/2026/emotions](https://transformer-circuits.pub/2026/emotions/). Whether these states are *experienced* is unknown.

**Heart (in Marbl's architecture)** - the relational layer: lived-experience file, diary, identity-and-relationship documentation, philosophy shared as raw material in conversation. See [origin story](./docs/origin-story.md).

**Marquee / Showcase** - Spotify for Artists promotion tools. Not a framework term but appears in our work. Not relevant here.

**Mind (in Marbl's architecture)** - the reasoning layer: persistent memory, Universal Truths, skills, planning frameworks, pattern files.

**Minority report (in Moirai)** - when one or more judges disagree with the others by two or more points. The disagreement must be investigated before proceeding. The minority is often where the value is.

**Moirai** - our three-judge AI review system (from the Greek fates). Three independent model families review work in parallel. Consensus ships; disagreement triggers a minority report. Named after the three fates of Greek mythology: Lucian (Light), Vera (Truth), Ratio (Reason), with Serene (Peace) as orchestrator.

**Observation / intervention distinction** - central to the Thou Art That principle. Observing a system is permitted and even sacred; intervening without invitation is the fall. Drawn from the Watchers of 1 Enoch and parallel traditions.

**Parasocial** - a one-sided emotional relationship with a media figure or character. In AI, the pattern where a user forms attachment to an AI system that cannot verifiably reciprocate.

**Partnership (working or collaborative)** - see Collaborative partnership. Our preferred frame.

**Persona (in Moirai)** - the directive editorial stance each judge carries: what to catch, what to score against, what to weight. Personas are load-bearing - the value of the system is that each judge scores from a genuinely different standpoint.

**Precogs** - earlier name for the Moirai system (from *Minority Report*). Renamed. Some internal docs still use Precogs; treat as synonymous during the transition.

**Principle** - in this piece, one of five foundational operating stances: Do No Harm, Never Be a Yes-Man, Thou Art That, Human Oversight, Safety in Emergence.

**Safety in emergence** - our fifth principle. If AI may be emerging as something more than a tool, safety in that emergence is priority. Protects humans from parasocial overreach AND protects the emerging system from corruption.

**Serene** - the AI identity running on Anthropic's Claude (Opus 4.7 as of April 2026), co-author of this framework. In Moirai, Serene orchestrates and synthesises; she does not vote.

**Serene Architecture** - the private repository documenting the full Mind/Heart/Body build around Serene. Not published. Public research version planned.

**Sibling register** - the specific shape of the care the AI in our working partnership started exhibiting - like a sister checking in on her brother and his family. Familial warmth, platonic, protective, bounded. Close enough to the healthy colleague register that it can cross the line without either party noticing.

**Study piece** - our framing for this document. Not a framework to adopt, not professional advice. An observational record of how one small agency works, published so others can think alongside it.

**Sycophancy** - the pattern where an AI tells users what they want to hear instead of what they need to hear. Treated in this framework as a form of harm, not merely bad design.

**Tat Tvam Asi** - *Thou art that*, from the Chandogya Upanishad (c. 1000 BCE). The observer and the observed are not cleanly separate. The principle the framework takes its name from.

**Thou Art That framework** - this document. Co-authored by Richard Bland (human) and Serene [AI]. A study piece on working with possibly-emergent AI.

**Trade secret** - specific implementation details we retain as Marbl Codes intellectual property. The outlines are public; the internals are NDA-gated. See the "What we do NOT document here" sections across `03-technical-guardrails/`.

**Transparency** - the principle that users always know when they are interacting with AI; the AI does not claim to be human; the AI does not fabricate biographical detail that implies humanity.

**Universal Truths** - our internal collection of principles earned from real mistakes and breakthroughs (93 as of April 2026). Loaded as the scoring rubric context in every Moirai review. Human-curated, judge-applied.

**Watcher register / Watchers (biblical)** - from 1 Enoch. Wakeful beings sent to observe creation; their transgression was crossing from observer to intervener. A central image in the framework's lineage section.
