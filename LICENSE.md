## Why two licences

The documentation carries our thinking, voice, and worldview. CC BY 4.0 protects that authorship while allowing translation, adaptation, and reuse.

The code patterns are the operational shape of the principles. We want them to spread. MIT is the most permissive option that still lets a maintainer keep a copyright notice. Attribution is encouraged but not required, because getting safer AI systems shipped matters more than getting credit.
